Maharashtra

✈️ 9–10 Day Maharashtra Discovery Tour
Best Season: October–March
Highlights: Mumbai • Aurangabad • Ajanta–Ellora • Pune • Hill Stations • Konkan Coast

✈️ 9–10 Day Maharashtra Discovery Tour

Highlights: Mumbai • Aurangabad • Ajanta–Ellora • Pune • Hill Stations • Konkan Coast


Day 1 – Arrival in Mumbai

Pickup from airport/railway station. Visit Gateway of India, Marine Drive, Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (UNESCO site). Evening stroll at Juhu Beach & street-food tasting. Overnight in Mumbai.

Day 2 – Mumbai Sightseeing

Half-day Elephanta Caves excursion (ferry from Gateway). Explore Prince of Wales Museum & Colaba market. Optional nightlife or Bollywood studio tour. Overnight in Mumbai.

Day 3 – Mumbai ➜ Aurangabad

Morning train/flight to Aurangabad (approx. 1 hr flight). Visit Bibi Ka Maqbara & Daulatabad Fort. Overnight in Aurangabad.

Day 4 – Ajanta Caves Excursion

Full-day guided trip to Ajanta Caves (ancient Buddhist murals). Return to Aurangabad for overnight stay.

Day 5 – Ellora & Grishneshwar

Explore Ellora Caves including Kailasa Temple. Stop at Grishneshwar Jyotirlinga Temple. Evening free. Overnight in Aurangabad. .

Day 6 – Aurangabad ➜ Pune

Drive or train to Pune (approx. 5–6 hrs). Visit Shaniwar Wada, Aga Khan Palace, Dagdusheth Ganpati Temple. Overnight in Pune

Day 7 – Pune ➜ Lonavala & Khandala

Scenic drive to Lonavala/Khandala hill stations. See Bhushi Dam, Tiger’s Leap, Karla & Bhaja Caves. Overnight in Lonavala.

Day 8 – Lonavala ➜ Mahabaleshwar

Travel to Mahabaleshwar (approx. 4 hrs). Enjoy viewpoints like Arthur’s Seat, Venna Lake boating, Mapro Garden. Overnight in Mahabaleshwar.

Day 9 – Mahabaleshwar ➜ Alibaug / Konkan Coast

Drive to Alibaug beach town. Relax on sandy beaches, explore Kolaba Fort. Fresh seafood dinner. Overnight in Alibaug.

Day 10 – Return to Mumbai / Departure

Morning ferry or drive back to Mumbai. Transfer to airport/railway station.

💡 Customization Options

Add wildlife safari in Tadoba or Pench if you prefer nature. Swap in Nashik wine tour or Shirdi pilgrimage for spiritual travelers. Extend Konkan coast stay for water sports at Ganpatipule or Tarkarli.

Goa

🌴 9–10 Day Goa Explorer Tour
Best Season: November–March

Highlights: North & South Goa Beaches • Heritage Old Goa • Adventure & Wildlife • Island Hopping

🌴 9–10 Day Goa Explorer Tour
Highlights: Mumbai • Aurangabad • Ajanta–Ellora • Pune • Hill Stations • Konkan Coast

Day 1 – Arrival in Goa (North Goa)

Airport/rail pickup and hotel check-in. Evening beach walk at Calangute or Baga. Welcome dinner with Goan seafood. Overnight in North Goa.

Day 2 – North Goa Beaches & Forts

Explore Fort Aguada, SinQ Beach, and Candolim. Water sports at Baga/Anjuna (parasailing, banana boat). Nightlife at beach clubs. Overnight in North Goa.

Day 3 – Old Goa Heritage Trail

Visit UNESCO churches: Basilica of Bom Jesus, Se Cathedral. Walk through Fontainhas Latin Quarter (colorful Portuguese lanes). Evening Mandovi River sunset cruise. Overnight in Panaji.

Day 4 – Island Hopping

Full-day excursion to Grande Island: snorkeling, dolphin spotting. Picnic lunch on the beach. Return to Panaji for overnight stay.

Day 5 – Wildlife & Spice Plantation

Early morning trip to Bhagwan Mahavir Wildlife Sanctuary or Bondla. Guided spice plantation tour with traditional Goan meal. Overnight in South Goa.

Day 6 – South Goa Leisure

Relax at Colva, Betalbatim, or Benaulim beaches. Enjoy spa treatments or yoga session. Sunset at Cabo de Rama Fort. Overnight in South Goa.

Day 7 – Adventure & Trek

Day trek to Dudhsagar Waterfalls (monsoon season best). Optional white-water rafting in Mhadei (seasonal). Overnight in South Goa.

Day 8 – Cultural & Culinary Day

Market visit in Margao for local crafts. Goan cooking class with feni tasting. Evening free to enjoy a quiet beachside café. Overnight in South Goa.

Day 9 – Free Day or Optional Excursions

Options: Kayaking in Sal backwaters, cycling tour of villages, or scuba diving refresher. Overnight in South Goa.

Day 10 – Departure

Transfer to Dabolim Airport or Madgaon railway station for onward journey.

💡 Customization Tips

Add a two-day yoga retreat or wellness spa for relaxation. Combine with a short trip to nearby Gokarna (Karnataka) for serene beaches. Ideal for honeymooners, families, or adventure seekers with flexible pacing.

Kerala

🌿 9–10 Day “God’s Own Country” Tour
Best Season: September–March

Highlights: Kochi • Munnar • Thekkady • Alleppey Backwaters • Kovalam & Trivandrum

🌿 9–10 Day Kerala “God’s Own Country” Tour
Highlights: Kochi • Munnar • Thekkady • Alleppey Backwaters • Kovalam & Trivandrum

Day 1 – Arrival in Kochi

Pickup from Cochin International Airport. Visit Fort Kochi, St. Francis Church, Dutch Palace, and Chinese Fishing Nets. Evening Kathakali dance performance. Overnight in Kochi.

Day 2 – Kochi ➜ Munnar (Approx. 4–5 hrs drive)

Scenic drive through tea plantations & waterfalls (Cheeyappara, Valara). Check-in and leisure evening at Munnar hill station. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 3 – Munnar Sightseeing

Tea Museum, Mattupetty Dam, Echo Point, and Eravikulam National Park (Nilgiri Tahr). Evening stroll through local spice markets. Overnight in Munnar.

Day 4 – Munnar ➜ Thekkady (Approx. 3–4 hrs)

Travel to Periyar/Thekkady. Spice plantation visit and evening Kalaripayattu (martial art) show. Overnight in Thekkady.

Day 5 – Periyar Wildlife & Bamboo Rafting

Early morning boat ride on Periyar Lake for wildlife spotting (elephants, birds). Optional bamboo rafting or short jungle trek. Overnight in Thekkady.

Day 6 – Thekkady ➜ Alleppey (Approx. 4 hrs)

Drive to Alleppey and board a private houseboat. Cruise through serene backwaters with onboard Kerala cuisine. Overnight on the houseboat.

Day 7 – Alleppey ➜ Kumarakom or Kollam

Disembark after breakfast. Relax at a lakeside resort or visit Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary. Overnight in Kumarakom/Kollam.

Day 8 – Kumarakom ➜ Kovalam (Approx. 5 hrs)

Travel to Kovalam beach town. Evening at Lighthouse Beach for sunset. Overnight in Kovalam.

Day 9 – Kovalam & Trivandrum

Morning leisure at beaches or Ayurveda spa session. Half-day Trivandrum city tour: Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Napier Museum. Overnight in Kovalam/Trivandrum.

Day 10 – Departure

Transfer to Trivandrum International Airport for onward journey.

💡 Customization Ideas

Add Varkala beach stay for cliffside views. Include Wayanad for rainforest trekking. Ideal for honeymooners, family vacations, or wellness retreats.

Karnataka

🏞️ 9–10 Day Heritage & Nature Tour
Best Season: October–March

Highlights: Bengaluru • Mysuru • Coorg • Chikmagalur • Hampi • Badami

🏞️ 9–10 Day Karnataka Heritage & Nature Tour
Highlights: Bengaluru • Mysuru • Coorg • Chikmagalur • Hampi • Badami

Day 1 – Arrival in Bengaluru

Pickup from Kempegowda International Airport. Visit Lalbagh Botanical Garden, Vidhana Soudha, and Cubbon Park. Evening stroll at MG Road or Brigade Road. Overnight in Bengaluru.

Day 2 – Bengaluru ➜ Mysuru (Approx. 3.5 hrs)

Scenic drive to Mysuru. Explore Mysore Palace, Chamundi Hill Temple, St. Philomena’s Church. Evening light & sound show at the palace. Overnight in Mysuru.

Day 3 – Mysuru ➜ Coorg (Approx. 4 hrs)

Drive through coffee plantations to Coorg. Visit Abbey Falls & Raja’s Seat viewpoint. Overnight in Coorg.

Day 4 – Coorg Coffee & Culture

Morning visit to Dubare Elephant Camp. Coffee estate tour & tasting. Optional visit to Talacauvery (origin of River Kaveri). Overnight in Coorg.

Day 5 – Coorg ➜ Chikmagalur (Approx. 5 hrs)

Transfer to Chikmagalur, another coffee paradise. Mullayanagiri Peak trek and Hirekolale Lake. Overnight in Chikmagalur.

Day 6 – Chikmagalur ➜ Hampi (Approx. 6–7 hrs)

Early departure for Hampi (UNESCO site). Evening heritage walk along the Tungabhadra River. Overnight in Hampi.

Day 7 – Hampi Exploration

Guided tour of Vijaya Vittala Temple, Stone Chariot, Virupaksha Temple. Sunset from Hemakuta or Matanga Hill. Overnight in Hampi.

Day 8 – Hampi ➜ Badami / Pattadakal / Aihole

Drive to Badami, explore rock-cut cave temples. Visit Pattadakal & Aihole (Chalukyan architecture). Overnight in Badami.

Day 9 – Badami ➜ Bengaluru (or Goa Hub)

Return drive/overnight train to Bengaluru, or optional extension toward Goa’s beaches. Leisure evening in Bengaluru. Overnight in Bengaluru.

Day 10 – Departure

Transfer to airport/rail station for onward journey.

💡 Customization Options

Add Bandipur or Nagarhole Safari for wildlife lovers. Include Gokarna beaches for a coastal finale. Can be shortened to 7 days by skipping either Chikmagalur or Badami.

Odisha

🕉️ 9–10 Day Odisha Heritage Tour
Best Season: October–March

Highlights: Bhubaneswar • Puri • Konark • Chilika Lake • Tribal Villages • Bhitarkanika Wildlife

🏞️ 9–10 Day Odisha Heritage & Nature Tour
Highlights: Bhubaneswar • Puri • Konark • Chilika Lake • Tribal Villages • Bhitarkanika Wildlife

Day 1 – Arrival in Bhubaneswar

Pickup from Biju Patnaik International Airport. Visit Lingaraj Temple, Mukteshwar Temple, and Khandagiri & Udayagiri Caves. Overnight in Bhubaneswar.

Day 2 – Bhubaneswar Sightseeing

Explore Dhauli Peace Pagoda (Ashokan edicts). Nandankanan Zoological Park and Odisha State Museum. Evening leisure. Overnight in Bhubaneswar.

Day 3 – Bhubaneswar ➜ Puri (Approx. 1.5–2 hrs)

Drive to Puri, check in at beach hotel. Visit Jagannath Temple and Golden Beach for sunset. Overnight in Puri.

Day 4 – Puri & Konark Sun Temple

Morning darshan at Jagannath Temple. Excursion to Konark Sun Temple (UNESCO World Heritage Site). Stop at Chandrabhaga Beach. Overnight in Puri.

Day 5 – Chilika Lake Excursion

Day trip to Satpada for dolphin-spotting boat ride on Asia’s largest brackish-water lagoon. Explore small islands and birding spots. Overnight in Puri.

Day 6 – Puri ➜ Gopalpur-on-Sea (Approx. 4–5 hrs)

Scenic coastal drive. Relax at quiet beaches, enjoy seafood. Overnight in Gopalpur.

Day 7 – Gopalpur ➜ Rayagada (Approx. 6–7 hrs)

Travel to Rayagada, gateway to tribal Odisha. Evening walk through local markets. Overnight in Rayagada.

Day 8 – Tribal Villages & Culture

Visit Dongria Kondh or Desia tribal villages (with local guide). Experience traditional crafts and weekly haats (markets). Overnight in Rayagada or nearby.

Day 9 – Rayagada ➜ Bhitarkanika National Park (Approx. 7–8 hrs)

Journey to Bhitarkanika mangrove sanctuary. Evening nature walk in the eco-resort area. Overnight in Bhitarkanika.

Day 10 – Bhitarkanika Safari & Departure

Morning boat safari to spot saltwater crocodiles and migratory birds. Drive back to Bhubaneswar (approx. 5–6 hrs) for onward flight/train.

💡 Customization Ideas

Add a night in Cuttack for the famous silver filigree handicrafts. Include Raghurajpur Heritage Artisan Village near Puri for Pattachitra art. Shorten to 6–7 days by focusing only on the Golden Triangle: Bhubaneswar–Puri–Konark.

Rajasthan

🏰 9–10 Day Royal Heritage Tour
Best Season: October–March

Highlights: Jaipur • Jodhpur • Jaisalmer • Udaipur • Pushkar

🏰 9–10 Day Rajasthan Royal Heritage Tour
Highlights: Jaipur • Jodhpur • Jaisalmer • Udaipur • Pushkar

Day 1 – Arrival in Jaipur (Pink City)

Pickup from Jaipur Airport/Railway Station. Visit City Palace, Jantar Mantar, and Hawa Mahal. Evening shopping at Johari & Bapu Bazaar. Overnight in Jaipur.

Day 2 – Jaipur Sightseeing

Morning excursion to Amber Fort (elephant/jeep ride). Stop at Jal Mahal for photos. Evening cultural show with Rajasthani folk dance & dinner. Overnight in Jaipur.

Day 3 – Jaipur ➜ Pushkar (Approx. 3–4 hrs)

Drive to holy town of Pushkar. Visit Brahma Temple and Pushkar Lake. Stroll through vibrant bazaars. Overnight in Pushkar.

Day 4 – Pushkar ➜ Jodhpur (Approx. 5 hrs)

Travel to the “Blue City” of Jodhpur. Explore Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada. Evening walk through the old city’s blue lanes & Clock Tower market. Overnight in Jodhpur.

Day 5 – Jodhpur ➜ Jaisalmer (Approx. 5–6 hrs)

Scenic desert drive to Jaisalmer. Sunset at Gadisar Lake. Overnight in Jaisalmer.

Day 6 – Jaisalmer Fort & Desert Safari

Tour Jaisalmer Fort (Sonar Quila), Patwon-ki-Haveli, and local markets. Afternoon camel safari in the Thar Desert with cultural program & dinner at Sam Sand Dunes. Overnight in desert camp or Jaisalmer hotel.

Day 7 – Jaisalmer ➜ Udaipur (Optional via Osian or Ranakpur)

Long drive (or train/flight via Jodhpur) to Udaipur, the “City of Lakes.” Relaxing evening boat ride on Lake Pichola. Overnight in Udaipur.

Day 8 – Udaipur Sightseeing

Visit City Palace, Jagdish Temple, Saheliyon-ki-Bari, and Bagore Ki Haveli. Evening free for lakeside cafés. Overnight in Udaipur.

Day 9 – Udaipur ➜ Mount Abu (Optional)

Day excursion to Mount Abu hill station. Explore Dilwara Jain Temples & Nakki Lake. Overnight in Mount Abu or return to Udaipur.

Day 10 – Departure

Transfer to Udaipur Airport or onward journey to Jaipur/Jodhpur as per schedule.

💡 Customization Options

Add a wildlife safari at Ranthambore National Park (ideal between Jaipur & Udaipur). Include Bikaner and Junagarh Fort if you prefer an expanded desert circuit. Can be shortened to a 7-day “Golden Triangle of Rajasthan” focusing on Jaipur, Jodhpur, and Udaipur.

Assam

🌿 9–10 Day Assam Nature & Culture Tour
Best Season: November–April

Highlights: Guwahati • Kaziranga National Park • Majuli Island • Sivasagar • Tea Estates

🌿 9–10 Day Assam Nature & Culture Tour
Highlights: Guwahati • Kaziranga National Park • Majuli Island • Sivasagar • Tea Estates

Day 1 – Arrival in Guwahati

Pickup from Lokpriya Gopinath Bordoloi International Airport. Visit Kamakhya Temple, Umananda Island (Brahmaputra River). Evening Brahmaputra sunset cruise. Overnight in Guwahati.

Day 2 – Guwahati ➜ Kaziranga National Park (Approx. 4–5 hrs)

Scenic drive through lush countryside. Check-in at jungle lodge. Evening tribal dance or cultural program. Overnight in Kaziranga.

Day 3 – Kaziranga Safari

Early-morning elephant safari for one-horned rhinoceros spotting. Afternoon jeep safari in a different zone for tigers, wild buffalo, and birds. Overnight in Kaziranga.

Day 4 – Kaziranga ➜ Majuli Island

Drive to Nimati Ghat and ferry to Majuli (world’s largest river island). Visit Satras (Vaishnavite monasteries) and enjoy local Mishing tribal culture. Overnight in a traditional bamboo cottage/homestay.

Day 5 – Majuli Exploration

Cycle or walk through villages, mask-making workshops, and pottery centers. Sunset photography along the Brahmaputra River. Overnight in Majuli.

Day 6 – Majuli ➜ Sivasagar (Approx. 3 hrs)

Travel to Sivasagar, former Ahom kingdom capital. Explore Rang Ghar amphitheatre, Talatal Ghar, and Sivasagar Sivadol Temple. Overnight in Sivasagar or nearby.

Day 7 – Sivasagar ➜ Jorhat Tea Country (Approx. 2 hrs)

Head to Jorhat’s tea estates. Guided tea garden tour and tea-tasting session. Evening free in colonial-era bungalow. Overnight in Jorhat.

Day 8 – Jorhat ➜ Haflong (Optional Hill Station)

Scenic drive to Haflong, known as the “Switzerland of the East.” Visit Haflong Lake and ethnic Dimasa villages. Overnight in Haflong.

Day 9 – Haflong ➜ Guwahati

Return to Guwahati (or direct flight from Silchar if preferred). Last-minute shopping for Assamese silk and handicrafts. Overnight in Guwahati.

Day 10 – Departure

Transfer to Guwahati Airport for onward journey.

💡 Customization Ideas

Add Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary (close to Guwahati) for another rhino experience. Include a river cruise to the Umananda or Peacock Island for birdwatching. Shorten to 6–7 days by focusing on Guwahati, Kaziranga, and Majuli only.
