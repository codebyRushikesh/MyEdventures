We're MyEdventures!

Plan less and more ‘go with the flow’ and just see where life takes you. With these intentions, Rushikesh Bahekar started Edventure in 2015. Since then, Edventure has grown into a tour operator and travel agent with 4 team members.

Edventure wants you to connect to the real India : meet the locals in Maharshtra, Rajasthan Orrisa, Assam or More places. explore the vibrant jungle and stay at unique hotels and lodges, all with their own personal story. We care for India’s exuberant nature and work with partners (our hotels and rental car providers) who attach great importance to sustainability, who intend to be frontrunners in this field.

When you travel with us, you avoid the touristic hotspots as much as possible. You can book your individual round trip and explore the country in your own tempo by driving a rental car, but you can also join one of our special yoga and meditation group trips.

We compile tailor-made trips for you, your wishes are central. Because we are living in india we know all destinations and accommodations from personal experience and we visit our hotel and other providers on a regular basis. In this way, we are able to help you compile your perfect holiday. The personal contact with our clients is what makes our work so fun!

Our Expertise in Mountain Climbing


Our Expertise in Mountain Climbing At MyEdventures, mountain climbing isn’t just a sport—it’s our passion and lifelong pursuit. Over the years, we’ve honed our skills across some of the world’s most challenging terrains, from rugged alpine routes to towering Himalayan peaks.

We Work with the Professional Water Explorers

Exploring the mysteries of water—whether it’s the deep blue ocean, hidden underground springs, or vast freshwater lakes—requires skill, precision, and a deep respect for nature. At our organization, we proudly collaborate with professional water explorers who dedicate their lives to uncovering the secrets of the world’s waterways.

We’re the Best Jungle Adventurers of Central India

Exploring the wild heart of Central India is more than just a journey—it’s an exhilarating adventure that connects us with nature’s raw beauty. From the dense sal forests of Kanha to the tiger trails of Pench and the mystical landscapes of Satpura, we’ve trekked, camped, and discovered hidden gems that few have seen.
